What we stand for

Four commitments

The principles behind every decision we make about what to publish, who can read it, and how we teach.

01

DānaGenerosity

All teachings free, and they will stay free. Sharing the Dharma is the practice, not a transaction.

02

Clarity

Plain English. We do not hide behind jargon, and we explain Pāli and Sanskrit terms when they are useful.

03

Authenticity

Grounded in the canonical sources of the Pāli, Chinese, and Sanskrit traditions — not in soft self-help.

04

Accessibility

No geography, no language tax, no paywall. The Dharma was meant to be heard.
