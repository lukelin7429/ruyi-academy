Ru-Yi Academy

Buddhist study and practice for English-speaking learners — taught in plain English, grounded in the classical tradition.

Currently in Pilot Ru-Yi Academy is in an invitation-only Pilot phase through 2027. Public enrollment is planned for 2028.

What we offer

Structured study of Buddhist teachings — sutras, doctrine, and meditation practice — adapted for learners whose primary language is English. Our materials are written, not translated, in English by teachers familiar with both the source tradition and the questions Western practitioners actually bring.

Who we serve

Western practitioners who want substance over surface — including former English teachers who have lived and taught in Taiwan, and others looking for a serious, accessible path into Mahāyāna study.
