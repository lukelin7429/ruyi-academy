What we offer

Structured study of Buddhist teachings — sūtras, doctrine, and meditation practice — adapted for learners whose primary language is English. Our materials are written, not translated, in English by teachers familiar with both the source tradition and the questions Western practitioners actually bring.

Who we serve

Western practitioners who want substance over surface — former English teachers who have lived and taught in Taiwan, second-generation overseas Chinese, and others looking for a serious, accessible path into Mahāyāna and Theravāda study.

What we stand for
DānaGenerosity
All teachings free, and they will stay free. Sharing the Dharma is the practice, not a transaction.
Clarity
Plain English. We do not hide behind jargon and we explain Pāli terms when they are useful.
Authenticity
Grounded in the canonical sources of the Pāli, Chinese, and Sanskrit traditions, not in soft self-help.
Accessibility
No geography, no language tax, no paywall. The Dharma was meant to be heard.
